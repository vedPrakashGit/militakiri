Starter: 0 points
(this cannot fall below zero, the points loss will not be charged, the possible moves are visible)

Beginner: 100 points
(from this rank status the points loss will be charged, the possible moves are visible)

Novice: 200 points
(from this rank status the possible moves are no longer visually displayed on the board)

Elite: 70.000 points

Master: 90.000 points

Grandmaster: 100.000 points and more
