The History of The Game

Militakiri originated from a collaboration of Bart Van Cannegem & Lieven D'hondt in the early 2000s.

The game has undergone many changes over the years.

Thousands of hours of testing preceded with the support of enthusiastic testers to balance the game.

So many years later, we have made the step to a professional activity with our game company.

A well thought out plan has led to a digital version.

Discover this game and enjoy conquering and defeating your opponent!
